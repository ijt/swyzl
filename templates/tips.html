Swyzl


Cryptograms are word puzzles where a secret message has been scrambled by replacing one letter with another consistently throughout. For example, "A" goes to "P", "B" goes to "H" and so on. The spacing and punctuation stay the same.


Here are some tips for solving these puzzles:

Look forCould be
Single lettersA or I
Contractions It's, isn't, don't, won't, can't, I'll, I've, I'm, I'd, he's, it's, didn't, shouldn't, wouldn't, couldn't, etc.
Four letter words that begin and end with the same letterThat, high, else, bomb, died, dead, etc.
Four letter words with the same two letters in the middle Many times the two letters in the middle are "e" or "o". There are exceptions such as "eggs" or "odds."
Words with discernible patterns There, where, even, ever, always, people, between, little, etc.
